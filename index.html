Harry Styles

About

Harry Styles is a British singer and songwriter. He was born on February 1, 1994 in the United Kingdom.

Career

Harry Styles first found fame from the music competition, The X Factor, in 2010. Although he (and the other members) auditioned as a solo artist on the television show, Harry followed the advice of Simon Cowell and became a member of boy band, One Direction. One Direction recorded five studio albums prior to taking an indefinite haitus. It was at this time that Harry began his solo singing career. Harry has been nominated for and won numerous music awards, including the 2021 Grammy Award for Best Pop Solo Performance.

Discography

Harry Styles

His self-titled debut album was released on May 12, 2017. The album was influneced by the music of the 1960s and 70s. It can be described as rock and Britpop.

Fine Line

Styles' second album was released on December 13, 2019. This album focused on breakups and the emotions surrounding hearbreak. The music can be described as pop rock, and indie pop.

Harry's House

The third studio album by Harry Styles, Harry's House was released on May 20, 2022. Harry wrote songs that were introspective and took inspiration from Japanese city pop genre. Styles also used synthesizers to create a more bright feel to the music.
